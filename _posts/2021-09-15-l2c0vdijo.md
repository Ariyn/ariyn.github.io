-N옵션.
-column-names.

{: .tractatus}
1. -N옵션

   ```shell
       mysql -N -e 'SELECT * FROM user;'
   ```

   1. -column-names

   2. Write column names in results.

   3. (Defaults to on; use --skip-column-names to disable.)

   4. N, --skip-column-names

   5. Don't write column names in results.

   6. 실수로 -n 옵션을 주게되면 다른게 적용되니 조심하자

   7. -n, --unbuffered

      1. Flush buffer after each query.
